About us

Caring is central to the work we do and defines our responsibility to those we serve. The promise of swastik pharma is in the promise that our work holds for health and life.

Swastik pharma is a business in transition. New and innovative business models are in motion to make the most of emerging opportunities. A new drive for growth today permeates every mind-set, process and techno-innovation within Swastik pharma.At Swastik pharma, we are looking at new ways of thinking, new ways of working and new ways to touch people’s lives.

Our Values

MISSION:We’re dedicated to fulfilling the promise of human potential, in all places, in all aspects and stages of life. We believe that health is the key to that promise. Because at our healthiest, we’re capable of achieving anything. And that’s why our pursuit of helping people achieve their best health at every life stage will never end. This is how we put that belief into practice each and every day.

VISION:We strive to earn the trust of those we serve by committing to the highest standards of quality, excellence in personal relationships, and behavior characterized by honesty, fairness and integrity.The philosophy of ‘Doing Well and Doing Good’, along with our core values of Knowledge, Action, Care & Impact have been constant in our journey and serve as guideposts to help us become the company we would like to be.
